FAQ

For Doctor

How to register for doctor?

Admin will mail for query, then doctor will give his info(i.e. Name, Email, Password), then admin wil give an user id and password to doctor for login.

How to change slot if needed?

If a doctor wants to change his slots he/she has to mail admin for this and admin will update the new slot.
For Patient

How to get appoinment?

Patient has to click on the button named get appoinment in doctor's profile seeing the availability of the doctor and then he/she has to fill up a form and submit for confirming appoinment on required slot.

How many doctors are enlisted in DR. DHAKA till now?

BMDC registered 500 doctors are enlisted in DR. DHAKA till now.
